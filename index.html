Stamp your euros to spread the word about #Occupy
Tweet

How to?

Download the A4 template. Print the first page. Tape your notes on the template and put in the feeder of your printer. Then just print the page 2 of the template.
Please share your pictures if you print and share these stamped notes! Tweet @OccupyEuro.
